Tea

A soothing and refreshing beverage made with tea leaves.

Ingredients:

  • Water
  • Tea leaves
  • Sugar (optional)
  • Milk (optional)
  • Ginger (optional)

Instructions:

Boil water with tea leaves and optional ingredients. Strain into a cup. Add milk and sugar if desired. Serve hot.

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Garlic

  • Salt and pepper
  • Herbs (basil, oregano)

White Sauce Pasta

A creamy pasta dish with a rich white sauce made from milk and cheese.

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Salt and pepper
  • Vegetables (optional)

Instructions:

Cook pasta. Make white sauce by melting butter, adding flour, and gradually adding milk. Add cheese and stir until melted. Combine with pasta and vegetables. Serve hot.

Chowmein

A popular stir-fried noodle dish with vegetables and sauces.

Ingredients:

  • Noodles
  • Vegetables (bell peppers, carrots, cabbage, etc.)
  • Garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Salt and pepper
  • Oil

Instructions:

Cook noodles. Stir-fry garlic and vegetables in oil. Add soy sauce, vinegar, salt, and pepper. Add noodles and stir-fry until well mixed. Serve hot.

Burger

A classic sandwich with a patty, lettuce, tomato, and other toppings.

Ingredients:

  • Buns
  • Patty (beef, chicken, veggie, etc.)
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Cheese (optional)
  • Condiments (ketchup, mustard, mayo)

Instructions:

Grill or cook patty. Assemble burger with patty, lettuce, tomato, onion, cheese, and condiments in a bun. Serve with fries.

Pizza

A popular dish with a dough base, tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza base(readymade)
  • Tomato or pizza sauce
  • Cheese
  • Toppings (pepperoni, vegetables, etc.)
  • Herbs (oregano, basil)

Instructions:

Spread tomato or pizza sauce on pizza base, add cheese and toppings. Bake in a preheated oven until crust is golden and cheese is melted. Garnish with herbs. Serve hot.

Upma

An Indian dish made from Semolina(suji) and vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • Semolina (suji)
  • Onion
  • Green chilies
  • Mustard seeds
  • Curry leaves
  • Vegetables (carrots, peas, etc.)
  • Salt
  • Water

Instructions:

Dry roast semolina. Add mustard seeds, onions, and chilies. Add vegetables and cook. Add water and bring to a boil. Gradually add suji, stirring continuously. Cook until water is absorbed. Serve hot.

Masala Dosa

A crispy rice-made dish filled with a spicy potato filling.

Ingredients:

  • Rice and urad dal (for dosa batter)
  • Potatoes
  • Onion
  • Green chilies
  • Mustard seeds
  • Curry leaves
  • Turmeric
  • Salt
  • Oil

Instructions:

Prepare dosa batter and ferment. Make potato filling with chopped onions, chilies, mustard seeds, and turmeric. Cook dosa on a hot , add filling, and fold. Serve with chutney and sambar.

Pulao

A flavorful rice dish cooked with spices and vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • Basmati rice
  • Mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, beans, etc.)
  • Onion
  • Garlic and ginger
  • Whole spices (cinnamon, cloves, bay leaf, etc.)
  • Salt
  • Oil or ghee

Instructions:

Chop whole spices, onions, garlic, and ginger. Add vegetables and rice. Cook with water until rice is tender. Serve hot.
